Dumb Jokes That Make Me Laugh

A guy and a girl were driving down the road when they accidentally ran over a rabbit.
Panicked, the guy grabs an aerosol can, jumps out, and rushes to the rabbit.
He sprays the rabbit from head to toe, in which it sprang back to life.
It hopped a few feet and turned back to wave at the guy and girl.
It hopped a few more feet and turned back to wave again.
Hopped again a few more feet, turned to wave once more.
"Wow, what was in that can???" asked the girl
The guy held the can up, with a glimmer in his eye
"Hare spray. Revives dead hare and gives it a continual wave."
